Payment Details

Please complete your payment via SBI Collect before submitting this form (for paid categories).

Open SBI Collect Portal
  1. Open SBI Collect (State Bank Collect) and proceed to the payment portal.
  2. Select category: Educational Institutes.
  3. Select state: Maharashtra.
  4. In the top-left search bar, type Indian Institute of Science Education and Research.
  5. Click the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research link that appears in the results.
  6. Choose payment category: NCIKS 2026 from the dropdown.
  7. Fill the form and select the correct participant category and fee.
  8. After payment, save and print the generated receipt, bring it to the venue, and share the receipt (UTR) number.

Free Access category selected: payment and UTR are not required. Please complete registration to attend conference lectures.

Upon successful submission, a confirmation email will be sent to your registered email address with further details.
